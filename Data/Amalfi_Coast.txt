Amalfi Coast - My Experience

During my trip to Italy, visiting the Amalfi Coast was one of the highlights. I had heard a lot about it before, but seeing it in real life was even more impressive. The combination of the blue sea, the dramatic cliffs, and the colorful houses built on the mountains made it feel like a dream.

One of the towns I visited was Positano. Walking there was not easy because of the steep streets and many stairs, but every time I stopped, I saw a beautiful view of the sea. I also spent some time near the beach, where I relaxed and enjoyed the atmosphere.

What I liked:
- The incredible sea views and fresh air
- The unique architecture with houses built on the hills
- Small local shops with handmade items

Food experience:
I tried seafood pasta in a small restaurant near the coast. It was very fresh and had a strong lemon flavor, which I later learned is typical for the region.

Problems:
- The area was very crowded, especially in the afternoon
- The roads were narrow and made traveling by bus a bit stressful
- Prices were higher compared to other places in Italy

Travel experience:
I used a bus at first, but later I preferred taking a boat between towns. It was much more relaxing and also gave a better view of the coastline.

Tips:
- Visit early in the morning to avoid crowds
- Wear comfortable shoes because of all the walking
- Try local food and avoid very touristy restaurants
- If possible, use boats instead of cars for transportation
